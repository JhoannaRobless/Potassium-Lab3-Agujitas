Valorant

is an online multiplayer computer game, produced by Riot Games. It is a first-person shooter game, consisting of two teams of five, where one team attacks and the other defends. Players control characters known as 'agents', who all have different abilities to use during gameplay.

5 reasons Why I love this game:

  • It is fun.
  • Heros are unique.
  • It relives stress.
  • You can play with friends.
  • Riot is generous now a days.
